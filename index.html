Bio

Currently a final-year Master's student in Electronics Engineering (Global Korea Scholar 2021) at Yeungnam University and doing research at Advanced Visual Intelligence Lab (AVIL) under Prof. Kim Sungho on Computer vision and AI. I have done internship under Prof. Xiatian Zhu on Deepfake. Completed my Korean Language course from Kangwon National University. I hold a Bachelor’s degree from Narula Institute of Technology and have prior research experience in geosciences, biomedical engineering, and automation, including a stint as a research intern at DRDO, DRL, under Scientist Dhiraj Dutta.
