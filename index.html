Bio

I am currently a final-year Master's student in Electronics Engineering at Yeungnam University, South Korea, under the guidance of Prof. Kim Sungho in the Advanced Visual Intelligence Lab (AVIL). As a 2021 Global Korea Scholarship (GKS) recipient, my research is primarily focused on computer vision and deep learning. I have collaborated on projects with prominent institutions such as the Electronics and Telecommunications Research Institute (ETRI), PHA in South Korea, and RLRC, with our work on the RLRC project receiving significant news coverage. Additionally, I am working with Professor Xiatian Zhu, Surrey University on Deepfake technology research.

Prior to my Master's, I earned a Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering from Narula Institute of Technology (NiT) under WBUT. My early research included geoscience studies under Dr. Dibyendu Sur, followed by projects in Biomedical Engineering, Machine Learning, and Automation with Prof. Bansari Deb Majumdar and Ms. Susumita Das. My hands-on experience in the field was further enhanced during my time as a research intern at the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO), DRL, under Scientist Dhiraj Dutta.
