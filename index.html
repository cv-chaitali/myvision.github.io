Bio

Currently first year PhD candidate in EECS department at DGIST, supervised by Professor Yeseong Kim. I did my MS in Electronics Engineering (Global Korea Scholar 2021) at Yeungnam University under the supervision of Prof. Kim Sungho on Computer vision and AI. I have done an internship under Prof. Xiatian Zhu on Deepfake. Completed my Korean Language course from Kangwon National University. I hold a Bachelor’s degree from Narula Institute of Technology and have prior research experience in geosciences, biomedical engineering, and automation, including a stint as a research intern at DRDO, DRL, under Scientist Dhiraj Dutta.

My current research interests include Generative AI, catastrophic forgetting, 3D vision, and multi-modal learning.
